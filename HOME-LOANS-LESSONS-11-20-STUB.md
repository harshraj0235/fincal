## Lessons 11-20 Content Summary:

**13. Co-Applicant** - 40-60% more loan, double tax benefits (₹7L vs ₹3.5L), women get extra discount

**14. Prepayment** - NO penalty for floating, save ₹1.8-2.2L per ₹1L prepaid, early years maximum impact

**15. Balance Transfer** - Transfer when 1%+ rate difference, ₹15L+ outstanding, save ₹5-10L

**16. Top-Up Loans** - Extra money at 9-10% vs 15% personal loan, up to 85% property value

**17. Insurance** - Property mandatory (0.25-0.50%), life optional but recommended for sole earner

**18. Valuation** - Bank values property, if lower than agreed you pay difference, get independent valuation first

**19. Common Mistakes** - Not comparing banks, EMI &gt;40% income, not reading agreement, plot-only loans

**20. Choose Best** - Compare rate + fees + flexibility + service, apply to 5 banks, negotiate 0.25-0.50%
