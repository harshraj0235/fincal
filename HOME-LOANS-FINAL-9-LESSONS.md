## 📋 LESSONS 12-20 - CREATING NOW:

### 12. Pre-Approval Process
- What: Conditional approval before property selection
- Benefits: Know budget, seller confidence, faster closing
- How: Submit basic docs, soft credit check
- Validity: 60-90 days
- Key: Not binding, actual approval after property selection

### 13. Co-Applicant & Joint Loans  
- 40-60% higher loan amount
- Both incomes considered
- BOTH claim tax benefits (₹7L vs ₹3.5L)
- Women co-applicant: Extra 0.05-0.10% discount
- Who: Spouse, parent, sibling

### 14. Prepayment & Foreclosure
- NO penalty for floating rate (RBI rule)
- 2-4% penalty for fixed rate
- Prepay ₹1L = save ₹1.8-2.2L interest
- Early years maximum impact (70-80% is interest)
- Strategy: Prepay during low rate periods

### 15. Balance Transfer
- When: 1%+ rate difference, ₹15L+ outstanding, 10+ years left
- Process: Apply new bank → sanction → transfer
- Cost: ₹5-10K transfer fee
- Savings: ₹5-10L over remaining tenure
- Example: ₹30L @ 9% → 8% = save ₹5.87L

### 16. Top-Up Loans
- Extra loan on existing home loan
- Up to 85% of current property value
- Interest: 9-10.5% vs 15% personal loan
- Uses: Education, medical, business, debt consolidation
- No tax benefit on top-up (unless for home renovation)

### 17. Home Loan Insurance
- Property insurance: MANDATORY (0.25-0.50% of loan)
- Life insurance: OPTIONAL (but recommended if sole earner)
- Cost: ₹5,000-15,000/year
- Coverage: Outstanding loan amount
- Benefit: Family protected if borrower dies

### 18. Property Valuation Process
- Bank's valuer inspects property
- Methods: Comparison, cost, income approach
- If bank value < agreed price: You pay difference!
- Example: Agreed ₹50L, bank values ₹45L → Need ₹14L down (not ₹10L)
- Solution: Get independent valuation BEFORE token

### 19. Common Home Loan Mistakes
1. Not comparing 5+ banks
2. Taking plot-only loan (no tax benefits!)
3. EMI > 40% of income
4. Not reading loan agreement
5. Choosing fixed over floating
6. Not claiming tax benefits
7. Being guarantor without tracking
8. Not prepaying in early years
9. Believing verbal promises
10. Not checking CIBIL before applying

### 20. How to Choose Best Home Loan
- Compare matrix: Rate (40%), Fees (20%), Flexibility (20%), Service (20%)
- Apply to 5 banks simultaneously
- Negotiate spread by 0.25-0.50%
- Check: Processing fee, prepayment terms, rate change frequency
- Read ALL terms before signing
- Choose bank with good customer service (20-year relationship!)
