**Key Points:**
- Section 80C: ₹1.5L on principal repayment
- Section 24(b): ₹2L on interest

- Joint loan: BOTH claim = ₹7L total!
- Calculator showing tax savings

### 13. Co-Applicant & Joint Home Loans ⏳
**Key Points:**
- 40-60% higher loan amount
- Double tax benefits (₹7L vs ₹3.5L)
- 0.05-0.10% rate discount
- Who can be co-applicant
- Women co-applicant advantage

### 14. Home Loan Prepayment & Foreclosure ⏳
**Key Points:**
- Prepay ₹1L = save ₹1.8-2.2L interest!
- NO penalty for floating rate (RBI rule)
- 2-4% penalty for fixed rate
- When to prepay: Early years maximum impact
- Prepayment calculator

### 15. Balance Transfer: When & How? ⏳
**Key Points:**
- Transfer when: 1%+ rate difference, ₹15L+ outstanding, 10+ years left
- Process: Apply to new bank, sanction letter, transfer fee ₹5-10K
- Savings calculator
- Real example: ₹30L outstanding, save ₹5.87L

### 16. Top-Up Home Loans Explained ⏳
**Key Points:**
- Extra loan on existing home loan
- Up to 85% of current property value
- Interest: 9-10.5% (vs 15% personal loan!)
- Uses: Education, medical, business, debt consolidation
- Example: ₹10L top-up vs personal loan comparison

### 17. Home Loan Insurance (Must or Skip?) ⏳
**Key Points:**
- Types: Life insurance, property insurance
- Cost: 0.25-0.50% of loan amount
- Mandatory property insurance
- Life insurance optional (but recommended if sole earner)
- Cost-benefit analysis

### 18. Property Valuation Process ⏳
**Key Points:**
- Bank's valuer inspects property
- Methods: Comparison, income, cost
- If valuation < agreement: You pay difference!
- Example: Agreed ₹50L, valued ₹45L → You need ₹14L down (not ₹10L)
- How to avoid: Independent valuation first

### 19. Common Home Loan Mistakes to Avoid ⏳
**Key Points:**
- Mistake 1: Not comparing 5+ banks
- Mistake 2: Ignoring processing fees/hidden charges
- Mistake 3: Taking plot-only loan (no tax benefits!)
- Mistake 4: EMI &gt; 40% of income
- Mistake 5: Not reading loan agreement
- Mistake 6: Choosing fixed over floating
- Mistake 7: Not claiming tax benefits
- Mistake 8: Not prepaying in early years
- Mistake 9: Believing verbal promises
- Mistake 10: Not checking CIBIL before applying
