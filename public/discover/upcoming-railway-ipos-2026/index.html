All Aboard the Bull Run! Top Railway PSU IPOs to Watch in 2026

By MoneyCal Team • 4 जुलाई 2026

The Golden Age of Railway Stocks

If there is one sector that has consistently minted wealth for retail investors in the Indian stock market recently, it is Railway Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs). Driven by the government's unprecedented capital expenditure (capex) push to modernize the Indian Railways, stocks like IRFC, RVNL, and IRCTC have delivered multi-bagger returns.

Capitalizing on this immense investor appetite, the government is preparing to unlock value in several other profitable, unlisted railway subsidiaries through Initial Public Offerings (IPOs) in 2026.

Advertisement

The IPO Pipeline: Who is Next?

  • Dedicated Freight Corridor Corporation of India (DFCCIL): One of the most anticipated IPOs. DFCCIL is executing the massive freight corridor project, which will revolutionize logistics in India. Its monopoly in this new infrastructure space makes it highly attractive.
  • Integral Coach Factory (ICF): The premier manufacturing unit responsible for producing the Vande Bharat express trains. With the government planning hundreds of new Vande Bharat trains, ICF’s order book is overflowing.
  • Centre for Railway Information Systems (CRIS): The IT backbone of the Indian Railways. Similar to IRCTC's tech dominance, CRIS handles ticketing infrastructure, freight operations management, and passenger information systems.

Why Are Investors So Bullish?

The bull case for Railway PSUs rests on two pillars: monopoly and massive government backing. These companies operate in a sector with virtually no private competition. Furthermore, the Union Budget continues to allocate record-breaking funds (exceeding ₹2.5 lakh crore annually) to railway infrastructure, ensuring robust revenue visibility for these PSUs for the next decade.

Additionally, PSU stocks generally offer attractive dividend yields, providing a steady income stream alongside capital appreciation.

Risk Warning: While the narrative is strong, investors must be cautious about valuations. Some listed railway stocks have run up too fast and are trading at premium valuations that may already price in future growth.

How to Prepare for the IPOs

Given the retail frenzy around PSU IPOs, expect massive oversubscription. Ensure your demat account is funded and consider applying through multiple family member accounts to increase allotment probability. Read the Red Herring Prospectus (RHP) carefully to understand the exact use of IPO proceeds.
