Top IPOs to Apply This Week (Hidden Gems Unlocked)

By MoneyCal Team • 4 जुलाई 2026

The Indian primary market is witnessing an unprecedented gold rush. As we close out June 2026, a tidal wave of Initial Public Offerings (IPOs) across both the Mainboard and SME segments is hitting Dalal Street, and retail investors are scrambling for allotments.

The SME Lottery Ticket

While Mainboard IPOs offer stability, the real frenzy is happening in the SME (Small and Medium Enterprises) space. Investors are chasing astronomical listing gains, often exceeding 50% to 100% on day one. But applying blindly is a recipe for disaster.

How to Spot a "Hidden Gem"

Don't let FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out) dictate your investing. Here is how smart money analyzes an IPO:

  • Use of Proceeds: Is the company raising money to build a new factory and expand (Good), or is it an Offer for Sale (OFS) where promoters are just cashing out (Bad)?
  • Valuation Check: Look at the Price-to-Earnings (P/E) ratio. If an unknown SME is demanding a higher P/E than the established market leader in its sector, it is likely overpriced.
  • Ignore the GMP: The Grey Market Premium is completely unregulated and easily manipulated by large operators. Base your decision on the Red Herring Prospectus (RHP), not WhatsApp rumors.

The Liquidity Trap

A word of caution for SME IPO applicants: The minimum application size is usually ₹1 Lakh to ₹1.5 Lakh, and post-listing liquidity can be extremely low. If the stock hits a lower circuit, you might be stuck holding the shares unable to sell.
