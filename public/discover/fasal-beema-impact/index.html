Fasal Beema ka Kisanon par Asar aur Krishi Arthvyavastha

By MoneyCal Team • 15 जुलाई 2026

Fasal beema yaani crop insurance, kisanon ke liye ek bahut hi mahatvapurna vikalp hai. Yeh unhe unki kheti ke liye suraksha pradan karta hai, khaaskar jab unke beej ya fasal ko kisi prakritik aapda jaise baadh ya sukhad ke karan nuksan hota hai. Fasal beema ke zariye, kisanon ki aarthik sthirata mein sudhar hota hai aur yeh krishi arthvyavastha ke liye bhi bahut zaroori hai.

Fasal Beema ka Prabhav

Kya aap jaante hain ki Bharat mein, 2020-21 ke dauran, 7.5 crore kisanon ne farming insurance ke liye apply kiya tha? Iska matlab hai ki desh ki 30% se zyada kheti is beema ke antargat aati hai. Iska upyog karne wale kisanon ko apne nuksan ka 50% tak pura bhugtan milta hai, jo unki aarthik sthiti ko sudharne mein madad karta hai.

Advertisement

Kisanon ki Aarthik Sthirata

Fasal beema ki wajah se kisanon ki aarthik sthirata mein sudhar hota hai. Ek survey ke anusar, 60% kisanon ne bataya ki unhone beema lene ke baad apne kharchon mein kami dekhi hai. Yeh beema unhe ek suraksha kavach pradan karta hai, jisse wo apne kharchon ko asani se sambhal sakte hain. Is wajah se, unki samriddhi mein bhi vriddhi hoti hai.

Agricultural Economy par Asar

Jab kisan financially stable hote hain, to iska seedha asar desh ki arthvyavastha par padta hai. Fasal beema ke zariye, kisano ki kharid shakti badhti hai, jisse ve naye beej, khad aur technology kharid sakte hain. Ek report ke anusar, kisano ke beema ki wajah se, Bharat ki agricultural GDP mein 10% tak ki vriddhi hui hai.

Advertisement

Kisano ke Liye Beema Yojnaayein

Bharat sarkar ne Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana shuru ki hai, jisse kisanon ko sasti aur asan beema suvidha mil sake. Is yojna ka 50% kharch sarkar uthati hai, jisse kisanon ko sirf 2% premium dena padta hai. Is yojna ka fayda lekar, 2021 mein 90 lakh kisanon ne apni faslon ko beema karwaya.

Samasyaayein aur Chunautiyan

Halaanki, beema lene mein kuch samasyaayein bhi hain. Jaise ki, kisanon ko beema leni ki prakriya ke baare mein puri jaankari nahi hoti. Ek survey se pata chala hai ki 40% kisanon ne beema lene se isliye mana kiya kyunki unhe iski samajh nahi thi. Isliye, yeh zaroori hai ki sarkar aur NGOs milkar kisanon ko beema ki mahatta samjhaayein.

Advertisement

Kisanon ka Samarthan

Aakhir mein, kisano ko beema lene ke liye prerit karna bahut zaroori hai. Kisanon ko unke haq aur beema ke labhon ke baare mein jaankari dena chahiye. Iske liye, Income Tax Calculator aur SIP Calculator jaise tools se bhi madad li ja sakti hai, jisse wo apne aarthik vikalpon ko samajh sakein.

Antim Vichar

Fasal beema kisanon ke liye ek zaroori suraksha kavach hai. Yeh unki aarthik sthirata ko badhata hai aur krishi arthvyavastha ko majboot banata hai. Har kisan ko is beema ka labh lena chahiye, taki wo apne bhavishya ko surakshit kar sakein.
