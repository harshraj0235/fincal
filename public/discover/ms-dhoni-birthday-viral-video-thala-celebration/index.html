MS Dhoni Birthday Viral Video: Thala’s Unseen Celebration Inside; Fans Emotional on Legendary Cricketer's Special Day!

By MoneyCal Team • 7 जुलाई 2026

The internet is officially painted yellow today as millions of fans across the globe celebrate the birthday of India's most beloved cricketing icon, Mahendra Singh Dhoni (MS Dhoni). Known affectionately as "Thala" by his die-hard Chennai Super Kings (CSK) fanbase, the legendary former captain's birthday has sparked a massive trend across social media platforms. An unseen viral video of his intimate birthday celebration has left fans highly emotional.

The Unseen Viral Celebration Video

While MS Dhoni is famous for staying away from the social media limelight, his wife Sakshi Dhoni recently shared a heartwarming reel giving fans a sneak peek into his special day. The viral video shows Dhoni in his signature simple avatar, cutting a customized cake surrounded by close friends, family, and his beloved pet dogs at his Ranchi farmhouse. The sheer simplicity of the celebration is winning hearts on the internet.
