New Skilling Programme: Upgrading ITIs for Industry Needs - Complete Guide

By MoneyCal Team · 12 जून 2024 · 10 min read मिनट पढ़ें

भारत सरकार ने Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) को modern industry की requirements के according upgrade करने के लिए एक comprehensive skilling programme launch किया है। यह programme youth को industry-ready skills provide करने और employment opportunities increase करने के लिए design किया गया है।

New Skilling Programme Overview

यह ambitious programme Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship के under चलाया जा रहा है। इसका main objective ITIs को modern technology, updated curriculum, और industry partnerships के साथ upgrade करना है ताकि students को relevant skills मिल सकें।

विज्ञापन

Programme Highlights

  • 15,000+ ITIs का nationwide upgradation
  • 200+ नए modern courses introduction
  • Industry 4.0 technologies में specialized training
  • International certification programs
  • 100% placement assistance guarantee
  • Entrepreneurship development support
  • Digital learning platforms integration
  • Industry mentorship programmes

Programme के तहत ITIs में state-of-the-art infrastructure develop किया जा रहा है:

विज्ञापन

  • Virtual reality training modules

  • Industry-standard testing equipment
  • Clean energy technology labs

Admission Process और Eligibility

Upgraded ITI courses में admission के लिए simple process follow करनी होती है:

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 8th pass for basic courses
  • 10th pass for advanced technical courses
  • 12th pass for specialized technology courses
  • Age limit: 14-40 years (category wise relaxation)
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Aadhar card और domicile certificate

Application Process

  • Online application through official portal
  • Document verification at selected ITI
  • Counseling session attendance
  • Course selection based on merit
  • Fee payment और admission confirmation
  • Classes start date notification

Financial Support और Scholarships

Government students को various financial support schemes provide करती है:

Scholarship Schemes

  • SC/ST students को 100% fee waiver
  • OBC students को 50% fee concession
  • Girls को additional 20% scholarship
  • Monthly stipend during training period
  • Free study materials और tools
  • Hostel accommodation support
  • Transport allowance for rural students
  • Merit-based additional scholarships

Placement Assistance Program

Programme का major attraction 100% placement assistance guarantee है:

Placement Support Features

  • Regular campus recruitment drives
  • Industry mentorship programs

  • Soft skills development sessions

  • Follow-up support for 2 years

  • International certification tie-ups
  • Digital badges और credentials
  • Continuous assessment based grading
  • Competency-based evaluation system

How to Apply

Interested candidates निम्नलिखित steps follow करके apply कर सकते हैं:

  • Official website visit करें: skillindiadigital.gov.in
  • Nearest upgraded ITI locate करें
  • Available courses की list check करें
  • Online application form fill करें
  • Required documents upload करें
  • Application fee payment करें
  • Counseling date और venue note करें
  • Regular updates के लिए portal check करते रहें

Conclusion

New Skilling Programme through ITI upgradation India की youth को modern skills के साथ empower कर रहा है। यह programme न केवल employment opportunities increase करता है बल्कि entrepreneurship को भी promote करता है। Industry 4.0 के इस era में यह programme youth को future-ready बनाने में crucial role play कर रहा है।

अगर आप skill development में interested हैं तो यह programme आपके लिए excellent opportunity है। आज ही nearest ITI से संपर्क करें और अपने career को नई दिशा दें। Government की इस initiative का benefit उठाकर आप भी successful career build कर सकते हैं।
