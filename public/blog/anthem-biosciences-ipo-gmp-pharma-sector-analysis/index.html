Anthem Biosciences IPO: 12% GMP Creates Investor Frenzy in Pharma Sector

By MoneyCal Team · 10 जुलाई 2025 · 10 मिनट पढ़ें

Anthem Biosciences has captured significant investor attention with its IPO generating a robust 12% Grey Market Premium, reflecting strong confidence in the pharmaceutical sector and the company's specialized bioscience capabilities. As India's pharmaceutical industry continues its global expansion and innovation drive, specialized players like Anthem Biosciences are positioned to benefit from the sector's growth trajectory.

Anthem Biosciences IPO: Key Details and Market Response

The IPO has garnered strong investor interest, reflected in the healthy Grey Market Premium and strong subscription numbers across all investor categories during the bidding process.

Company Overview: Specialized Bioscience Solutions

Anthem Biosciences operates as a leading Contract Research, Development and Manufacturing Organization (CRDMO) specializing in small molecule drug development and biocatalysis solutions for global pharmaceutical companies.

विज्ञापन

Business Segments and Capabilities

  • Contract Research: Drug discovery and development services
  • Process Development: Scale-up and manufacturing optimization
  • Biocatalysis: Enzyme-based synthesis for pharmaceutical intermediates
  • Custom Manufacturing: API and intermediate production
  • Analytical Services: Quality control and regulatory testing
  • Regulatory Support: Documentation and filing assistance

Financial Performance: Strong Growth Trajectory

Anthem Biosciences has demonstrated consistent financial growth with improving profitability margins, reflecting the company's operational efficiency and strong demand for its specialized services.

  • EBITDA Margin: 32.5% (industry-leading margins)

  • Return on Equity: 24.8%
