New Fund for First-Time Women/SC/ST Entrepreneurs: ₹2 Cr तक का Loan - Complete Guide 2025

प्रथम बार महिला/SC/ST उद्यमियों के लिए नया फंड: ₹2 करोड़ तक का ऋण - संपूर्ण गाइड 2025

🟢 Active Women Empowerment 📅 Launched: 2025-03-08 💰 Budget: ₹10,000 करोड़ (2025-26) 👥 Beneficiaries: 2 लाख entrepreneurs (target)
New Fund for First-Time Women/SC/ST Entrepreneurs 2025 offers ₹2 crore collateral-free loans with 3-5% interest subsidy. Includes mentorship, incubation support, and 100% government guarantee.

✅ लाभ (Benefits)

  • Collateral-free loans up to ₹2 crore for first-time entrepreneurs
  • Interest rate subsidy of 3% for women entrepreneurs
  • Additional 2% rate concession for SC/ST women
  • Mentorship and incubation support included
  • 100% guarantee coverage by government
  • Grace period of 12 months for loan repayment

📋 पात्रता (Eligibility)

  • First-time women entrepreneurs (never taken business loan)
  • SC/ST category entrepreneurs (male/female both eligible)
  • Age between 21-45 years at time of application
  • Minimum educational qualification: Class 12th
  • Valid business idea with market potential
  • Commitment to create at least 3 direct employments

📄 आवश्यक दस्तावेज़ (Documents)

  • Educational certificates and mark sheets
  • Caste certificate (for SC/ST applicants)
  • Detailed business plan and project report
  • Bank account statements (6 months)
  • Identity proof (Aadhaar/Passport/Voter ID)
  • Address proof and residential certificate

📝 आवेदन प्रक्रिया (How to Apply)

  1. Register on Stand-Up India portal or visit branch
  2. Fill detailed application with business proposal
  3. Upload required documents and certificates
  4. Attend mandatory entrepreneurship training program
  5. Present business plan to evaluation committee
  6. Receive loan sanction and mentorship support
🌐 Official Website 📞 Helpline: 1800-180-1111

Application Process: Step-by-Step Detailed Guide

Application process को deliberately user-friendly बनाया गया है, खासकर उन applicants के लिए जो पहली बार business loan apply कर रहे हैं।

Phase 1: Pre-Application (2 सप्ताह पहले)

  • 📝 Business idea को clearly define करें
  • 📊 Market research conduct करें
  • 💡 Unique Selling Proposition (USP) identify करें
  • 📈 Financial projections prepare करें
  • 🎯 Target customer segment analyze करें
  • 📋 Required licenses और permits की list बनाएं
  • 🏢 Business location finalize करें

Phase 2: Documentation Preparation (1 सप्ताह)

  • 📄 Educational certificates organize करें
  • 🏷️ Caste certificate obtain करें (यदि applicable)
  • 🏦 Bank statements collect करें (minimum 6 months)
  • 🆔 All identity documents verify करें
  • 🏠 Address proof और residential certificate arrange करें
  • 📊 Detailed business plan draft करें
  • 💼 Professional references prepare करें

Phase 3: Online Application Submission

  • 🌐 Visit www.standupmitra.in/women-sc-st-fund
  • 📝 Create account with valid email और mobile number
  • 📋 Fill detailed application form (takes 45-60 minutes)
  • 📤 Upload all required documents (PDF format, max 5MB each)
  • ✅ Review application thoroughly before submission
  • 🔄 Get application reference number
  • 📧 Confirmation email receive करें

Phase 4: Evaluation और Interview Process

  • 📞 Initial screening call (within 7 days)
  • 🎓 Mandatory training program enrollment
  • 👥 Business plan presentation (evaluation committee के समक्ष)
  • 🏢 Field visit और verification (यदि required)
  • 💼 Mentor assignment और first meeting
  • ✅ Final approval communication
  • 📜 Loan agreement signing और disbursement

राजस्थान के tribal area से राजू मीणा ने ₹1.2 करोड़ से handicraft export business शुरू किया। Traditional tribal art को international market में पहुंचाकर उन्होंने 100+ artisans को employment दी है। अब 15 countries में export कर रहे हैं।

❓ अक्सर पूछे जाने वाले सवाल (FAQ)

क्या यह fund केवल women entrepreneurs के लिए है?

नहीं, यह fund first-time women entrepreneurs और SC/ST category के सभी entrepreneurs (male/female दोनों) के लिए है। हालांकि women को कुछ additional benefits मिलते हैं।

Maximum loan amount कितनी है?

Women entrepreneurs के लिए ₹2 करोड़ और SC/ST male entrepreneurs के लिए ₹1.5 करोड़ तक का loan available है।

Interest rate क्या है?

Women के लिए 6-9% (3% subsidy के बाद) और SC/ST categories के लिए additional 2% concession मिलती है, जिससे effective rate 4-7% हो जाती है।

क्या collateral की जरूरत है?

नहीं, यह complete collateral-free loan है। Government द्वारा 100% guarantee दी जाती है।

Application process कितने दिन में complete होती है?

Complete application submit करने के बाद 45-60 दिन में loan sanction हो जाता है, बशर्ते सभी documents proper हों।
