Kosi-Mechi River Linking in Bihar 2025: Impact on Local Farmers & Finances

बिहार में कोसी-मेची नदी जोड़ने की योजना 2025: स्थानीय किसानों और वित्त पर प्रभाव

🟢 Active Agriculture & Farmers 📅 Launched: 2025-05-01 💰 Budget: ₹8,000 करोड़ (2025-30) 👥 Beneficiaries: 15 लाख+ किसान
Kosi-Mechi River Linking project 2025 transforms Bihar agriculture with improved irrigation, flood control, and enhanced farm income opportunities.

✅ लाभ (Benefits)

  • Improved irrigation facilities
  • Flood control and management
  • Enhanced agricultural productivity
  • Year-round water availability
  • Increased crop diversity options
  • Better farm income prospects

📋 पात्रता (Eligibility)

  • Farmers in Kosi-Mechi region
  • Land ownership or lease documents
  • Agricultural activities for minimum 2 years
  • Valid farmer registration
  • Bank account for benefit transfers

📄 आवश्यक दस्तावेज़ (Documents)

  • Land ownership documents
  • Farmer registration certificate
  • Aadhaar Card and bank details
  • Previous crop cultivation records
  • Water usage permissions
  • Flood damage certificates (if applicable)

📝 आवेदन प्रक्रिया (How to Apply)

  1. Register with district irrigation department
  2. Submit application for water allocation
  3. Complete soil and water testing
  4. Receive irrigation schedule and guidelines
  5. Implement water-efficient farming practices
  6. Monitor crop yield improvements
