PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana 2025: Free 300 Units Electricity for 1 Crore Households

पीएम सूर्य घर मुफ्त बिजली योजना 2025: 1 करोड़ घरों को 300 यूनिट मुफ्त बिजली

🟢 Active Urban Development 📅 Launched: 2024-08-15 💰 Budget: ₹75,000 करोड़ (5 वर्षीय योजना) 👥 Beneficiaries: 1 करोड़ घर
PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana provides 300 units free electricity monthly through rooftop solar installation. Get up to 60% subsidy on solar panels. Complete guide for 2025 with application process and benefits.

✅ लाभ (Benefits)

  • 300 units free electricity per month
  • Rooftop solar installation subsidy up to 60%
  • Net metering facility for excess power sale
  • Reduced electricity bills by 80-90%
  • Clean and renewable energy source
  • Low maintenance solar systems

📋 पात्रता (Eligibility)

  • Own residential property with suitable rooftop
  • Minimum rooftop area of 100 sq ft required
  • Valid electricity connection
  • No pending electricity dues
  • Structural stability of rooftop certified
  • Clear land title documents

📄 आवश्यक दस्तावेज़ (Documents)

  • Property ownership documents
  • Electricity bill copy
  • Aadhaar Card of property owner
  • Bank account details
  • Structural stability certificate
  • Site layout and rooftop photographs
  • NOC from housing society (if applicable)

📝 आवेदन प्रक्रिया (How to Apply)

  1. Visit PM Surya Ghar official portal
  2. Register with mobile number and Aadhaar
  3. Fill online application with property details
  4. Upload required documents
  5. Site inspection by empaneled agency
  6. Technical feasibility assessment
  7. Installation and commissioning
  8. Net meter installation and grid connection
