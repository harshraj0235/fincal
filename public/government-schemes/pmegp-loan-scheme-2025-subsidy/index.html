PMEGP Loan Scheme 2025: Get Up To ₹50 Lakh Loan with 35% Subsidy

पीएमईजीपी ऋण योजना 2025: 35% सब्सिडी के साथ ₹50 लाख तक का ऋण प्राप्त करें

🟢 Active Business & Startups 📅 Launched: 2008-08-15
The Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) provides hefty subsidies to help unemployed youth set up new micro-enterprises.

✅ लाभ (Benefits)

  • Bank loan up to ₹50 Lakh for manufacturing sector
  • Bank loan up to ₹20 Lakh for service/business sector
  • Government subsidy from 15% to 35% on project cost
  • Boosts employment generation in rural and urban areas

📋 पात्रता (Eligibility)

  • Individuals above 18 years of age
  • At least VIII standard pass for projects costing above ₹10 lakh (manufacturing) and ₹5 lakh (service)
  • SHGs, institutions registered under Societies Registration Act

📄 आवश्यक दस्तावेज़ (Documents)

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Project Report (DPR)
  • Education/Skill Certificate
  • Category Certificate (if applicable)

📝 आवेदन प्रक्रिया (How to Apply)

  1. Apply online on the PMEGP e-portal (kviconline.gov.in)
  2. Fill application form and upload documents
  3. Application is scrutinized by District Level Task Force (DLTF)
  4. Approved applications are sent to financing banks
🌐 Official Website

PMEGP is a major credit-linked subsidy scheme by the Ministry of MSME that helps in generating self-employment opportunities through the establishment of micro-enterprises in the non-farm sector.
