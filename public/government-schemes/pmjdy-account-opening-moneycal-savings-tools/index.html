Complete guide to opening PMJDY account in 2025 with step-by-step process, eligibility criteria, and how to use Moneycal.in tools for savings calculation and financial planning.

✅ लाभ (Benefits)

  • Zero balance account opening facility
  • Free RuPay debit card with accident insurance
  • Overdraft facility up to ₹10,000 after 6 months
  • Direct benefit transfer from government schemes
  • Life insurance cover of ₹30,000
  • Access to micro-credit and financial services

📋 पात्रता (Eligibility)

  • Indian citizen of any age
  • No existing bank account in any bank
  • Valid identity proof document
  • Valid address proof document
  • Minimum age requirement varies by bank

📄 आवश्यक दस्तावेज़ (Documents)

  • Aadhaar Card (preferred)
  • Voter ID Card
  • PAN Card
  • Driving License
  • NREGA Card
  • Passport Size Photographs

📝 आवेदन प्रक्रिया (How to Apply)

  1. Visit nearest bank branch or Business Correspondent
  2. Fill PMJDY account opening form
  3. Submit required documents for KYC verification
  4. Provide biometric verification if required
  5. Receive account number and debit card
  6. Activate account with first deposit
