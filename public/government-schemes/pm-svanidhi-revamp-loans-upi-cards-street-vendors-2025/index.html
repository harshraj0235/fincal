PM SVANidhi Revamp 2025: Loans, UPI Cards & Growth Tips for Street Vendors

पीएम स्वनिधि रिवैम्प 2025: स्ट्रीट वेंडर्स के लिए लोन, UPI कार्ड और ग्रोथ टिप्स

🟢 Active Urban Development 📅 Launched: 2025-01-20 💰 Budget: ₹700 करोड़ (2025-26) 👥 Beneficiaries: 50 लाख+ स्ट्रीट वेंडर्स
PM SVANidhi Revamp 2025 offers enhanced support to street vendors with loans up to ₹50,000, UPI cards, and digital payment incentives.

✅ लाभ (Benefits)

  • Collateral-free loans up to ₹50,000
  • Digital payment incentives
  • UPI QR code and card facility
  • Business development support
  • Insurance coverage
  • Credit score improvement

📋 पात्रता (Eligibility)

  • Street vendor with valid certificate
  • Operating business for at least 1 year
  • Aadhaar and bank account mandatory
  • No previous loan defaults
  • Age between 18-65 years

📄 आवश्यक दस्तावेज़ (Documents)

  • Street vendor certificate
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank account details
  • Business photographs
  • Identity and address proof
  • Mobile number verification

📝 आवेदन प्रक्रिया (How to Apply)

  1. Visit nearest lending partner bank
  2. Fill PM SVANidhi application form
  3. Submit required documents
  4. Bank verification process
  5. Loan approval and disbursement
  6. Receive UPI card and training
