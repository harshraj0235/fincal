ROI Calculation और Break-even Analysis

Return on Investment (ROI) और break-even point calculate करना business viability के लिए जरूरी है।

ROI Formula: (Net Profit / Total Investment) × 100

Break-even Formula: Fixed Costs / (Selling Price per Unit - Variable Cost per Unit)

  • Target ROI: 25-30% annually for small businesses
  • Break-even period: 8-15 months ideal
  • Payback period: 2-4 years reasonable
  • Gross margin: 40-60% for sustainable business
  • Net margin: 10-20% after all expenses
  • Cash conversion cycle: Minimize for better flow
