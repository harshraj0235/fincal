Confused between different types of loans? This tool helps you understand and compare common loan options available in India, such as Personal Loans, Home Loans, and Car Loans. Each loan type serves a different purpose and comes with its own set of interest rates, tenures, and eligibility criteria.

Common Loan Types:

  • Personal Loan: Unsecured, for various personal needs (medical emergencies, weddings, travel). Higher interest rates, shorter tenure.
  • Home Loan: Secured against property, for buying or constructing a home. Lower interest rates, longer tenure.
  • Car Loan: Secured against the vehicle, for purchasing a car. Moderate interest rates, medium tenure.
  • Education Loan: For financing higher education. Often comes with a moratorium period.

Loan Comparison Tips

  • Don't just compare interest rates; look at the total cost of the loan including processing fees and other charges
  • Consider prepayment options and penalties when choosing a loan
  • For long-term loans like home loans, even a small difference in interest rate can lead to significant savings
  • Check your eligibility before applying to avoid rejection which can impact your credit score
