Financial scams are rampant, and fraudsters constantly evolve their tactics. This section helps you identify common phishing attempts, fake SMS messages, and other financial frauds. Remember, banks or legitimate institutions will NEVER ask for your PIN, OTP, CVV, or full card number over the phone, SMS, or email.

Common Scam Tactics:

  • Phishing: Fake emails/SMS impersonating banks, asking for sensitive details.
  • OTP Fraud: Tricking you into sharing OTPs, which are then used to authorize transactions.
  • Vishing (Voice Phishing): Fraudulent calls pretending to be bank officials, asking for details.
  • Job Scams: Offering fake jobs and asking for 'processing fees' or bank details.
  • Lottery Scams: Notifying you of a lottery win and asking for a fee to release funds.
  • QR Code Scams: Sending QR codes that, when scanned, initiate a 'send money' transaction instead of 'receive money'.

What to do if you suspect a scam:

  • Do NOT click on any links.
  • Do NOT share any personal or financial details.
  • Block the sender.
  • Report to your bank immediately.
  • Report to cyber crime authorities (e.g., National Cybercrime Reporting Portal: cybercrime.gov.in).

Real-World Scam Examples

"Dear customer, your SBI account will be blocked today. Please update your KYC by clicking on [fake-link.com]"

⚠️ Scam: Banks never send links for KYC updates via SMS

"Congratulations! You have won ₹10,00,000 in Kaun Banega Crorepati lottery. To claim prize, pay ₹5,000 processing fee to [account details]"

⚠️ Scam: Legitimate lotteries never ask for fees to release winnings

"I'm calling from HDFC Bank. Your card has suspicious transactions. Share your OTP to block your card immediately."

⚠️ Scam: Banks never ask for OTPs over phone calls

Red Flags to Watch For

  • Urgent action required or account will be blocked
  • Requests for PIN, OTP, CVV, or password
  • Suspicious links or attachments
  • Poor grammar or spelling errors
  • Offers that seem too good to be true
  • Pressure to act immediately
  • Requests to download unknown apps
  • Calls asking for remote access to your device
  • Messages about winning contests you never entered
  • Requests to pay via unusual methods
