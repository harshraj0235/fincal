Fee Decoder: Uncover Hidden Bank Charges

Banks sometimes levy various charges that can go unnoticed in your transaction SMS or bank statements. These can include SMS alert charges, ATM transaction fees beyond free limits, minimum balance non-maintenance charges, debit card annual fees, and more. This tool helps you identify common fee-related keywords in your bank messages.

How to Use:

Paste a bank transaction SMS or a relevant part of your statement below. The tool will highlight common phrases indicating bank charges.

Common Bank Charges to Watch Out For

  • SMS Alert Charges
  • Minimum Balance Non-Maintenance Charges
  • ATM Transaction Fees (beyond free limits)
  • Debit Card Annual Maintenance Fees
  • Cheque Book Issuance Charges
  • NEFT/RTGS/IMPS Transaction Charges
  • Forex Markup Fees (for international transactions)

How to Minimize Bank Charges

  • Maintain minimum balance requirements
  • Use in-network ATMs to avoid fees
  • Opt for digital statements to avoid paper statement fees
  • Consider zero-balance or basic savings accounts
  • Review your bank's schedule of charges annually
  • Compare bank charges across different banks
  • Negotiate fee waivers based on relationship value

Always review your bank statements carefully and contact your bank if you have questions about any charges.
