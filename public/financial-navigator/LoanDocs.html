Document Preparation Tips

  • Keep all documents updated and readily accessible
  • Ensure all photocopies are clear and legible
  • Self-attest all photocopies with your signature
  • Carry original documents for verification
  • Organize documents in a folder for easy reference

Common Reasons for Loan Rejection

  • Incomplete or incorrect documentation
  • Poor credit score (below 700)
  • High existing debt burden
  • Unstable employment history
  • Insufficient income for requested loan amount

Always confirm the exact document requirements with your chosen lender, as lists can vary. Having all documents ready can significantly speed up your loan application process.
