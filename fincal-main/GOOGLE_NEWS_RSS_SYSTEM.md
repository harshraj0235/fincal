## 🔧 **TECHNICAL DETAILS:**

### **Google News RSS Format:**
```xml
<rss>
  <channel>
    <item>
      <title>Nifty 50 Hits 21,000 - Economic Times</title>
      <link>https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/...</link>
      <pubDate>Sat, 02 Nov 2025 10:30:00 GMT</pubDate>
      <description>Markets rally on strong earnings...</description>
    </item>
  </channel>
</rss>
```

### **Our Processing:**
1. Parse XML → Extract title, link, date, source
2. Clean title (remove "- Source Name" suffix)
3. Pass to AI: "Write unique analysis on this topic"
4. AI generates original 1000+ word article
5. Not plagiarism - completely new content!
